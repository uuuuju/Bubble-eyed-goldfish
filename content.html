Bubble Eyed Goldfish

About me

HI! I am the bubble eyed goldfish. I prefer to be called the Bubble Eye. I am a small variety of fancy goldfish with upward-pointing eyes that are accompanied by two large fluid-filled sacs, which are responsible for my adorable look xD.

My Characterestics

  1. I look cute
  2. I have metallic scales
  3. I usually like to eat fresh flake food.
  4. My bubbles are quite delicate, so I am careful not to go near sharp objects
  5. To be particular, I have a double tail and no dorsal fin
  6. Fun fact: I know HTML and CSS. xD
